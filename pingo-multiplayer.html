📖 HOW TO PLAY PINGO

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Be the first player to complete 3 complete rows (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win PINGO!

🎮 GAMEPLAY

  • Click an image to preview it in full screen
  • Click "Found It!" to confirm and mark with a ✓
  • Click a marked image to unmark it (only if you marked by mistake)
  • Each player has a unique shuffled board
  • All players share the same pool of marked images
  • When you mark an image, it syncs to everyone in real-time
  • Your board layout stays the same even if you refresh the page

🏆 WINNING

Complete 3 full rows from any combination of:

  • 5 Horizontal rows
  • 5 Vertical rows
  • 2 Diagonal rows

When someone wins, everyone sees the PINGO celebration!

🔒 SESSION CODES

  • The session creator gets a unique 6-character code
  • Share this code privately (text, email, etc.)
  • Other players must enter the code to join
  • Sessions are invite-only for privacy

🔀 CONTROLS

  • Shuffle My Board: Rearrange your images (only before game starts)
  • Reset Marks: Clear ALL marks for everyone (only before game starts)
  • Leave Session: Exit and return to main menu

⚠️ FAIR PLAY

  • Once anyone marks the first tile, the game is locked
  • Shuffle and Reset buttons disappear after the first mark
  • You can only play to win or leave - no cheating!

💡 TIPS

  • Keep your session code safe and private
  • Watch the "Players online" count to see who's joined
  • The center square has a golden border - special spot!
  • You can close and rejoin anytime - your progress is saved
  • Refresh the page if needed - your board stays the same!
