📖 HOW TO PLAY PINGO

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Be the first player to complete 3 complete rows (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win PINGO!

🎮 GAMEPLAY

  • Click images on your board to mark them with a ✓
  • Each player has a unique shuffled board
  • All players share the same pool of marked images
  • When you mark an image, it syncs to all players in real-time
  • Once anyone marks a tile, Shuffle and Reset buttons disappear - play to win or leave!

🏆 WINNING

Complete 3 full rows from any combination of:

  • 5 Horizontal rows
  • 5 Vertical rows
  • 2 Diagonal rows

When someone wins, everyone sees the PINGO celebration!

🔀 CONTROLS

  • Shuffle My Board: Rearrange your images (only before game starts)
  • Reset Marks: Clear ALL marks (only before game starts)
  • Leave Session: Exit and return to main menu

💡 TIPS

  • Share your session code with friends to play together
  • Watch the "Players online" count to see who's joined
  • The center square has a golden border - special spot!
