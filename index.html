📖 HOW TO PLAY PINGO

🎯 WHAT IS PINGO?

PINGO is Picture BINGO! Instead of numbers, you match photos on a 5×5 grid. Be the first to complete 3 full rows to win!

🚀 GETTING STARTED

  1. Create a Session: Click the "➕ Create New Session" card
  2. Choose a City/Theme: Select from available photo sets
  3. Share the Code: You'll get a 6-character code - send it to friends privately
  4. Start Playing: Once everyone joins, start marking photos!

🎮 GAMEPLAY BASICS

  • Each player gets a unique shuffled board with the same 24 photos
  • Click a photo to preview it, then click "Found It!" to mark
  • When you mark a photo, it syncs to all players instantly
  • Get 3 complete rows (any direction) to win PINGO!

🏆 WINNING CONDITIONS

Complete any 3 rows from:

  • 5 Horizontal rows
  • 5 Vertical rows
  • 2 Diagonal rows

🔒 PRIVACY & SECURITY

  • Sessions are invite-only - you need the code to join
  • Codes are not shown publicly in the session list
  • Only share codes with people you want to play with

⚠️ FAIR PLAY RULES

  • Before the first mark: You can shuffle your board or reset marks
  • After first mark: Game is locked - play to win or leave!
  • No cheating allowed - everyone plays by the same rules

💡 PRO TIPS

  • 🎨 Try different cities/themes for variety
  • 👥 More players = more fun competition!
  • 📱 Works great on phones, tablets, and computers
  • 🔄 You can rejoin anytime - your progress is saved
  • 📸 Look carefully - some photos look similar!
